feijoada

History

Feijoada (english: bean stew) is a classic from Brazil with a rather sad background. Feijoada started as a food for the slaves, as that the ingredients are rather cheap and back in the day they used the left overs of pork and beef recipes. Mixed with beans and other ingredients it is a wonderfull, filling and nutricious recipe for anyone who enjoys a warm meal.

ingredients

  • Beans
  • Onions and Garlic
  • Bacon
  • Salted beef and pork
  • Orange
  • Oil
  • bay Leaves
  • Salt and Pepper

Order of preparation

  1. Soak both beans and Meat
  2. Boil meat for 20 minutes
  3. Cook beans and meat
  4. Saute the bacon and aromatics
  5. thicken the stew
