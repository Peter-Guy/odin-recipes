Paçoca

History

A beloved snack for any party of afternoon tea. Paçoca is a simple yet delicious snack to make. WIth origins during colonial Brazil , but getting the recipe from the natives, brazilians came up with such candy.

Ingredients

  • peanuts
  • sugar
  • salt

Order of Preparation

  1. Add peanuts, sugar and a bit of salt to a blender
  2. blend until all becomes a type of cookie dough
  3. Roll in small portions of 1 - 2 tabble spoons
